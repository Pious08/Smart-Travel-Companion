# Smart Travel Companion - Market Listing

## App Details
**Name:** Smart Travel Companion

**Short Description:** Your all-in-one AI-powered travel guide and itinerary planner.

**Full Description:** Smart Travel Companion is designed to take the stress out of world exploration. From real-time weather updates and live currency conversion to personalized safety ratings for cities worldwide, this app ensures you have all the data you need at your fingertips. Pin your favorite destinations, track your hotel locations on interactive maps, and stay updated with smart flight reminders. Whether you are a solo backpacker or planning a family vacation, travel smarter with a companion that remembers your preferences and keeps you organized.

## Key Features
**Personalized Dashboard:** View pinned destinations and access a "Quick Start" guide for new users.

**Real-Time Data:** Integrated Weather and Currency APIs for live destination updates.

**Interactive Mapping:** Visualize saved hotels and nearby attractions via Google Maps integration.

**Smart Notifications:** Custom alerts for flight status, itinerary reminders, and exclusive travel deals.

**User Preferences:** Full support for Dark Mode and unit toggles (Metric/Imperial).
